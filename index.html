Phở

Phở is a famous Vietnamese noodle soup made with rice noodles, herbs, and beef or chicken. It is one of the most popular dishes in Vietnam.

Bánh Mì

Bánh Mì is a Vietnamese sandwich that blends French bread with Vietnamese fillings such as grilled pork, pâté, and pickled vegetables.

Gỏi Cuốn (Spring Rolls)

Gỏi Cuốn are fresh spring rolls made with rice paper, shrimp, herbs, and noodles. They are served with a peanut dipping sauce.
